# <span style="color: green;">1. Product Overview</span>

**The InHand ER615 is a versatile edge access router designed to seamlessly connect stores and offices to the network via 5G/4G cellular or wired options, ensuring business continuity and efficient operations. With gigabit Ethernet ports and 3000 Mbps Wi-Fi 6 LAN capabilities, the ER615 delivers exceptional network performance and high availability for a wide range of digital terminals.**

**Features and Advantages:** 
- **SD-WAN:** Combined with InCloud Manager, quickly builds SD-WAN networking for branch interconnection with flexibility and cost efficiency
- **Flexible Connectivity:** Wired broadband, 4G/5G, and Wi-Fi with link backup and load balancing
- **Wi-Fi 6 AX3000:** 802.11 ax/ac/a/b/g/n, dual-band 3000 Mbps for superior wireless performance
- **Centralized Management:** InCloud Manager for zero-touch deployment, remote upgrades, and visualized monitoring
- **Cost-Effective:** All-in-one solution for retail, office, and branch networking
