# <span style="color: green;">1. Product Overview</span>

**The FWA02 offers global 5G connectivity, ensuring convenient, reliable, and secure internet access in urban, suburban, remote, or rural areas. It delivers a faster, lower-latency, and higher-performance network experience with plug-and-play simplicity.**

**Product Positioning:** Instant 5G Connectivity Starts Here

**Features and Advantages:** 
- **Plug and Play High-Speed 5G:** Up to 4.76 Gbps DL / 1.25 Gbps UL, LTE CAT 19 fallback
- **Always-on Connectivity:** 5G/wired backup, cellular failover, dual SIM switching, optional eSIM
- **Secure Network Connection:** Firewall policies, access control, built-in VPN, data encryption, enhanced Wi-Fi security
- **Cloud-Managed:** InCloud Manager for zero-touch deployment, real-time monitoring, centralized management
- **Wi-Fi 6:** Dual-band 2.4 / 5.8 GHz, 3600 Mbps, 802.11 ax/ac/b/g/n
